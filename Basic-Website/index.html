Welcome to EDC IIEST Shibpur

Under the guidance of the Department of Human Resource Management, we facilitate interactions among students, faculty, professionals, entrepreneurs, mentors, and investors. We aim to foster a vibrant entrepreneurial ecosystem that nurtures innovation and business opportunities.
